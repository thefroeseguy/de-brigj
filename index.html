Integration and Inclusion through Information and Education

Welcome!

De Brigj informs, entertains, and ministers to the Low German community of Aylmer and surrounding areas through its programming. Listeners can stay current on news from Mennonite communities locally and abroad, traffic, weather, community events, and public service announcements. Listeners are also offered entertainment and companionship through music, humour, and ministry segments. De Brigj strives to uphold its mandate of integration and inclusion through information and education.
